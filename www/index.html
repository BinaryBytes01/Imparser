Imparser

HOW TO

Open Wireshark, start listening on your Internet Connection and filter for:

udp.port == 22023 && data.data[0] == 01 && data contains 40:1c:46:00 && frame contains "username"

(Make sure to replace username with your username first)

Select the package and right-click on the hex dump which appears at the bottom of the window and copy the Bytes "...as a Hex Stream".

Paste the copied hex dump into the text field below and press "Parse package".
